How to Apply for Trade Courses in Australia

  1. Choose your course and institution (TAFE, private colleges, universities).
  2. Prepare documents (passport, transcripts, English test scores).
  3. Apply through a registered migration/education agent.
  4. Receive your Confirmation of Enrolment (CoE).
  5. Apply for a Student Visa (Subclass 500).

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Can international students study trade courses in Australia?

Yes. Trade courses (VET) are open to international students with a valid student visa.

What is the cheapest trade course in Australia?

Some hospitality and cookery courses can start from AUD $8,000 per year, depending on the institution and location.

Do trade courses in Australia lead to PR?

Yes. Many courses such as Carpentry, Commercial Cookery, and Automotive Mechanic are on the PR occupation list.
